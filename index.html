About gzyuans

A small studio with large intent.

We started gzyuans in 2019 with a single belief: software should feel calm. Not minimalist for the sake of minimalism, but respectful of attention, intent, and the people who use it.

Today we are a distributed team of designers, engineers, and researchers, working with founders, public institutions, and product teams who care as much about how things work as how they feel.

Remote-first Privacy by default Open source Carbon neutral
How we work

From intent to interface
in four movements.

Step 01

Discover

Two-week sprint to map the problem space, audit existing systems, and align on success metrics.

Step 02

Design

Rapid prototypes, design tokens, and a living style guide — every decision explained and reversible.

Step 03

Build

Production-ready code, instrumented from day one. Continuous delivery with feature flags and observability.

Step 04

Operate

Quarterly review, dependency hygiene, and a partnership model that grows with your team.

Featured work

One project, in detail.

The shape of an engagement, from the first audit to the handover doc.

Fintech Web 14 months

Northwind Console — operator UI for B2B treasury.

A 14-month engagement to design and build the day-to-day control surface for Northwind's treasury product. The brief: replace a 12-tab spreadsheet workflow with something a tired operator could use at 2am without a manual.

We embedded a team of three with theirs for the duration, instrumented the rebuild from week one, and shipped a private beta in 11 weeks. GA in Q2 2026.
