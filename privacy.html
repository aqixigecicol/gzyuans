Privacy policy

Last updated · June 1, 2026 · Effective immediately

Short version

We collect almost nothing. We don't use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or session-replay tools. We don't sell, rent, or share personal data with anyone — for any reason. The only data we hold is what you give us directly (e.g. a contact form submission or an email thread), and we keep it only as long as we need to do the work you hired us for.

This document spells out the details in plain language. If anything below is unclear, please email us at privacy@gzyuans.com.

Who we are

The data controller for this website and our services is:

gzyuans, Lda.
Rua das Flores 42, 1200-194 Lisbon, Portugal

Email: privacy@gzyuans.com

For data-protection questions, you can also reach our Data Protection Officer at the same address, with the subject "DPO".

What we collect

The data we collect falls into three categories:

1. Data you give us

  • Information you submit through the contact form (name, email, company, message)
  • Information you send us by email or in the course of an engagement
  • Information you provide during a project kickoff, design review, or research call

2. Data we collect automatically

  • Server logs (IP address, user agent, requested URL, response code) — kept for 30 days for security and debugging
  • Aggregated, first-party page-view counts (we use Plausible Analytics with no cookies and no cross-site tracking)

3. Data we do not collect

  • No third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
  • No advertising or marketing trackers (Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight, etc.)
  • No session replay or heatmap tools (Hotjar, FullStory, etc.)
  • No social-media embed scripts that fingerprint visitors
  • No fingerprinting, ever

Why we collect it

We process personal data on the following legal bases under the GDPR:

  • Performance of a contract — when you hire us for a project, we process the data needed to deliver it
  • Legitimate interests — for basic server logs, security, and preventing abuse
  • Consent — for anything optional, such as newsletter subscriptions

How long we keep it

  • Contact form submissions: 24 months, after which we delete or fully anonymise them
  • Project data and correspondence: duration of the engagement + 7 years (for tax and contractual record-keeping)
  • Server logs: 30 days
  • Aggregated analytics: indefinitely, but with no personal identifiers

Who we share it with

Almost no one. Specifically:

  • Hosting provider — Vercel, Inc. (Frankfurt region) for the website; Supabase, Inc. for any project databases we host for clients
  • Email provider — Fastmail Pty Ltd (Australia) for inbound and outbound email
  • Payment processor — Stripe Payments Europe Ltd (Ireland) for invoices
  • Accountants & lawyers — as required for tax filings and legal compliance

We do not transfer personal data to any other third parties. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. We do not use data for advertising.

Cookies & tracking

This website does not set any marketing, advertising, or cross-site tracking cookies. The only browser storage we use is:

  • A first-party preference cookie (e.g. to remember your dark/light mode) — purely functional, no third parties
  • LocalStorage entries set by this site to remember UI state (e.g. "newsletter dismissed")

You can clear these at any time from your browser settings. We do not honour Do Not Track signals separately because we don't track you in the first place.

Your rights

Under the GDPR (and equivalent laws), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Portability of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the Portuguese CNPD or your local supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@gzyuans.com. We respond within 30 days, usually much faster.

How we protect it

We take security seriously. The data we hold is protected by:

  • TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest
  • Hardware security keys (FIDO2) for all production access
  • Quarterly third-party penetration testing of production systems
  • Encrypted backups, retained for 30 days, stored in a separate region
  • Annual security training for the entire team
  • A responsible disclosure programme with a 90-day disclosure window

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date
  • Email active clients and newsletter subscribers
  • For significant changes, request renewed consent where required by law
